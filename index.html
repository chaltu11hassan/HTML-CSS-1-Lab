Welcome, Coffee lovers!

Fun Fact: Did you know we came to love coffee because of some goats? Apparently, some guy tending to his goats noticed his animals became livelier after eating a certain berry. He boiled the berries to make the first coffee. Here we are today, acting like some goats and enjoying our coffee!
